*Super Lotto tickets are the only one's purchasable for $1.00

Step 1: Find a store near you

There are plenty of stores that sell lottery tickets. These of which include:

  • Convienance Stores
  • Gas Stations
  • Liquor Stores
  • Grocery Stores

Step 2: Pick Your Numbers

Next, choose five white ball numbers and one "red" ball number.

Depending on the type of lotto you choose, the requirements on which numbers can be chosen will vary.

PowerBall

Players can choose 5 (white ball) numbers from 1 to 69 and 1 PowerBall® number from 1 and 27. Choose your numbers using the play slip or ask the cashier to have the numbers randomly generated for you.

PowerBall® tickets cost $2.00 per play.

Mega Millions

Players can choose 5 (white ball) numbers between 1 and 70 and 1 Mega from 1 to 25. The numbers can also be randomly generated if the player would like to select the Quick Pick® option.

Mega Millions® tickets cost $2.00 per play.

Super Lotto

Players can choose 5 (white ball) numbers between 1 and 47 and 1 Mega number between 1 and 27. They may also randomly select numbers by selecting Quick Pick®, which will randomly choose the numbers for you.

Super Lotto® tickets cost $1.00 per play.

Step 3: Wait For the Drawing Date

Once you have purchased your ticket(s), look out for the ticket's drawing date. At the time of the drawing, the winning numbers will be displayed on the ticket's website. This is where you will check to see if the numbers you've picked match those of the winning ticket's.
